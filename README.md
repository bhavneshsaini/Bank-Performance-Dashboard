# Bank-Performance-Dashboard
🏦 Bank Branch Performance Overview — Power BI Dashboard

An interactive Power BI dashboard that provides a complete financial performance view of bank branches across India — covering revenue, deposits, loans, expenses, and NPA risk.

📈 Charts & Visuals

1. 📊 Total Revenue by State (Horizontal Bar Chart)

Compares state-wise revenue. West Bengal (3.3M) leads, followed by Maharashtra (3.2M), Telangana, Delhi, Tamil Nadu (~3.1M each), and Gujarat (3.0M).

2. 📉 Deposits & Loans by Month (Line Chart)

Tracks monthly trends. Deposits peak at 147M in February and decline to 97M by July. Loans remain stable between 85M–100M. Both lines suggest seasonal patterns around the financial year-end.

3. 📊 Total Revenue by Region (Vertical Bar Chart)


🥇 South — 9.0M
🥈 North — 8.5M
🥉 West — 6.2M
East — 5.9M


4. 🍩 Revenue by Branch (Donut Chart)

Revenue is evenly distributed (~10–11% per branch). Salt Lake is the top branch at 3.25M (10.99%), followed by Andheri, Banjara Hills, Connaught Place, and Anna Nagar.

5. 🥧 Deposits by Branch Manager (Pie Chart)

Sneha Patel leads with 165M (10.96%), followed by Rakesh K. (156M), Vivek Rao (152M), and others — all within a close range, showing balanced manager performance.

6. 🔽 City Slicer (Interactive Filter)

Filter all charts by city — Ahmedabad, Bengaluru, Bhubaneswar, Chandigarh, Chennai, Hyderabad, or Kolkata — for city-level drill-down analysis.


💡 Key Insights


⚠️ NPA (47.87M) exceeds Total Revenue (29.59M) — a risk flag worth monitoring
📅 February shows peak deposits, likely due to financial year-end tax investments
🏆 South region and Salt Lake branch are top performers
🔄 Branch-level revenue is well-balanced — no over-reliance on a single branch
